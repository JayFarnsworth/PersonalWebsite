Where I'm From

I grew up in Boulder, CO. I spent most of high school making movies and playing video games. I became a pro at filming and editing my own pictures, which has proved to be a valuable skill that I've yet to use once after graduating.

My Education

I attended Arizona State for three years, where I majored in Political Science. I originally wanted to be a lawyer, I guessed. I mostly hung around pools and spent time anywhere within 500 miles that wasn't class. This would explain the whopping 30 credits I received in my time there.

I transferred to CU Boulder following a solid session of getting my life together. I plowed through the other 3/4 of my degree in 2 years. I met many other bro/ hipster/ nerd/ wook friends to round out my collection.

Work
